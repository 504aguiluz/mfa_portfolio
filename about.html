I'm Matt Aguiluz, a recording engineer turned software developer from New Orleans, LA.

Logic and programming have always been exciting to me. I started with synthesizers and drum machines when I was young, then evolved into using the recording studio as a programmable instrument. Now, my medium is software and web applications.

I'm new to this career path, but I find it endlessly fascinating. The vector of logic and creativity exist here deeper than any other medium I've experienced. To bring soul and connection to others through software development is a beautifully evolved concept to me, both personally and for us as a species.

Here are a few things about me that I'd like to share with you aside from my engineering background.
